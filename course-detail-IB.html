The Bachelor of International Business [Hons] programme was established to produce students who are knowledgeable and able to conduct business in the international arena. Thus, the root of knowledge in business and management disciplines is essential preparation for facing a more competitive in the business world whether in private or public organizations particularly the multinational corporations, non-governmental organizations (NGOâ€™s), small-and-medium enterprises (SMEs) and other business entities and business service providers.

Bachelor of Business (International Business) with Honours

The objective of this program is to produce graduates with comprehensive knowledge and advanced skills to manage business operations on an international platform, in alignment with Malaysiaâ€™s growing prominence in the global trade landscape. This includes fostering expertise as managers or professionals capable of excelling in diverse business environments. To achieve this, an in-depth understanding of global issues and international business principles is essential, providing a strong foundation to navigate an increasingly competitive business world.

Graduates from this program will possess a deep understanding of the intricacies of business management both in Malaysia and on a global scale. They will also be well-versed in cultural, linguistic, political, geographical, and socio-economic factors that influence international business dynamics. Furthermore, graduates are equipped to integrate and apply their acquired knowledge and skills, demonstrate exceptional competence, independence, and problem-solving abilities through innovative and creative thinking, while upholding high ethical standards and commendable values.

Admission requirement

STPM Graduates

Fulfil the general requirement of the University and obtained at least Grade C (NGPM 2.0) at STPM level in any one (1) of the following subjects:

Science / Literature Stream:
Additional Mathematics, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Business Studies, Accounting, Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

and

Obtained at least credit (Grade C) in the Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) level in the following subjects:
i) English, and
ii) One (1) of the following subjects:
Mathematics, Additional Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering Drawing, Engineering Technology, Civil Engineering Studies, Mechanical Engineering Studies, Electrical & Electronic Engineering Studies, Accounting Principles, Basic Economics, Trade, Entrepreneurship Studies and Business Accounting.

and

Obtained at least Level 2 (Band 2) in the Malaysia University English Test (MUET) according to the validity period from January 2016.

and

Candidates do NOT have limb disabilities that make learning difficult.
