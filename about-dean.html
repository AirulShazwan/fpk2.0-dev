DEAN MESSAGE

Welcome to the Faculty of Business & Communication, Universiti Malaysia Perlis

A warm welcome to the Faculty of Business & Communication website. We are greatly honoured to have your interest in getting to know the Faculty of Business & Communication in UniMAP through the internet. We hope this website will be the gateway to the information regarding our faculty and open doors to its resources. This website will play the role as our virtual front desk in the World Wide Web to all stakeholders.

For your information, UniMAP has been recognised for the excellence in engineering research by the Government of Malaysia. Following UniMAP’s strong commitment to entrepreneurship, we also strive to provide a world class learning environment and training that emphasise entrepreneurship qualities which the students will acquire upon graduating.

We invite you to learn more about our faculty and look forward to meeting up with you. Just give us a call and together we shall schedule your visit to our faculty. Come and see for yourself what makes us distinctly different!

Associate Professor Dr. Mohammad Rezal Hamzah
Dean of Faculty Business & Communication, Universiti Malaysia Perlis
