Frequently Ask Questions (FAQ)

What are the programs offered by the Faculty of Business & Communication?

Faculty of Business and Communication (FPK) offers four undergraduate programs for undergraduate students to pursue their thoughts in New Media Communication, Interactive Media Technology, International Business and Engineering Entrepreneurship.

FPK also offer postgraduate studies which cover 1 master by coursework programme (Master of Business Administration) and 2 programme Master by Research which are Master of Humanities (Religion) and Master of Science (Research Programme) and Doctor of Philosophy in the field of Human Science that cover various subfields.

How can potential students search for potential supervisors?

In the website staff directory, the lecturers are organised according to specific departments and each lecturers’ area of interest are provided for reference.

What are the admission requirements for postgraduate programs?

Admission requirements include a bachelor’s degree in a relevant field, meeting minimum academic qualifications, and fulfilling English proficiency criteria if necessary.

How can I contact the faculty for more information?
You can contact the faculty via phone, email, or visit the campus. Contact details are provided on the official website under the ‘Contact Us’ section.
